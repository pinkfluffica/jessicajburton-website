Movidiam

Details

In April 2014 I worked on the product Movidiam, an app developed by Pendragon Productions. The project was an app for video producers to assemble a team, check in with team progress, manage a budget and showcase video projects, as well as maintain a profile page.

Working using the Bootstrap framework I had to heavily modify the standard Bootstrap CSS to match a design created in Adobe Illustrator. The majority of my time was taken up creating modals for the signup/profile edit process.

In the course of this work I discovered many things about styling HTML forms whose elements are notoriously difficult to style. The text input and checkbox styling can be seen in the second image. The final image shows one of the Illustrator files with 24 modal pop-ups and eight pages. In two weeks I completed approximately 18 modal screens.
